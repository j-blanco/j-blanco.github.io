THE MAN BEHIND THIS

Rafael Mendiola is a UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer born in the Philippines. His love for creativity first started at a young age when he started drawing his favorite super heroes and created new ones with his imagination.

He has specialized skills that includes visual design, user experience design, user interface design and front-end development. He has been involved in the entire product life cycle from idea to launch from his previous projects.

He is also a graduate of Social and Behavioral Sciences in Los Angeles Pierce College in California which he took because of his interest on analyzing user’s behavior; and would help him work with clients better and meet user’s interests. Albeit his line of chosen field, his love for creativity and the need for creative problem solving that comes along with everything from working with clients and users to designing and coding a website made him more driven and passionate to still pursue a career in digital design.

With his love for digital design and a passion to grow in this field, he studied special courses in Web Design & Development, Image Editing & Compositing, Illustration & Desktop Publishing and Digital Video Production. Along with over 7 years of design experience, vast knowledge of working on people’s interests, creative passion, hunger for learning and curiosity; he is always driven to take it to the next level as a person who is doing what he loves to do best.

Be part of his creativity and enjoy. He loves to join your team. Hire him for another creative and innovative designs!

PS: Rafael is also a basketball enthusiast thus, he loves playing basktetball during his free time.
